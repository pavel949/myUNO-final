# myUNO — Positioning

**One line.** myUNO is the one platform for serviced living in Phuket — it runs a residence's whole life: **stay, live, own**.

**Category.** An operating layer, not a hotel brand or a management company. Like Shopify for a store — you don't hire it, you run on it. That is why it is “[Project] **on** myUNO”.

**The core idea.** Everyone else owns one slice. **myUNO owns the whole life of the residence** — guest → buyer → owner, in one place.

**Differentiators.** The whole lifecycle, not one slice · fit to how property is actually owned here (owned units in existing projects; capital-light) · one place for everyone who touches a residence (the verified replacement for the Telegram group) · trust as infrastructure (Ombudsman-anchored, verified, protected).

**What it is NOT.** Not a hotel brand · not a channel manager · not a real-estate agency · not a Telegram group.

**Per audience.** Developers: your project operated to a serviced-apartment standard and worth more — brand intact. Owners: fully handled, earning more, and you always know how. Guests: a stay you can trust, everything around it in one place. Buyers: buy with real operating data — managed and earning from day one.

**Tagline.** Your one place.
