Brand Direction

Ignatev Estate · the platform
myUN

Your one place.

The trusted operating layer for serviced living in Phuket — the one place where everything about a residence is real, verified, and handled. Booking, living, services, community, ownership.

The idea

my+UNO
= your one

UNO means one. One system of record. One place for everything. One continuous loop. One identity across every role.

The name says the strategy: the platform exists to make five scattered things one. And my makes it personal — not a system, but yours.

Wordmark

myUN

On light

“my” set light and recessed — humble, personal. “UNO” solid — the anchor. The final O is the mark.

myUN

On dark

One word, always. Not “My Uno”, not “MYUNO”. Lowercase “myuno” only for domains and handles.

The mark

One ring, one point

  • One / whole — the single place, complete.
  • You, at the centre — the gold point is the person: your unit, your stay.
  • The loop — a quiet echo of the cycle the whole business turns on.

Voice

  • Calm, not loud. It never shouts or sells. Confidence is quiet.
  • Plain, not clever. Say the true thing simply. “Handled.” “Yours.” “In one place.”
  • Warm, not chummy. Personal and human, but always premium.
  • “One” runs through it. One place, all in one, your one.
